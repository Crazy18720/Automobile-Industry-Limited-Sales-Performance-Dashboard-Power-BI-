# Automobile-Industry-Limited-Sales-Performance-Dashboard-Power-BI-
This project analyzes sales, revenue, and performance metrics for an automobile company. The goal was to provide management with insights into vehicle sales, customer preferences, revenue growth, and regional performance. The dashboard enables business leaders to track key metrics, optimize sales strategy, and improve profitability.

**Project Overview:**
**1. Data Cleaning & Preparation**

Imported raw automobile sales data into Power BI.

Handled missing values and standardized product, category, and region fields.

Corrected data types (dates, revenue, units sold).

Removed duplicates and created consistent naming conventions.

**2. Data Modeling**

Built relationships between Sales, Customers, Dealers, and Products tables.

Added a Date table for time intelligence (YoY, MoM analysis).

Created calculated columns and DAX measures for KPIs (Revenue, Sales, Avg Price).

**3. Analysis & KPIs**

Sales & Revenue → Total Revenue, Units Sold, Avg Selling Price.

Time Analysis → Monthly, Quarterly, and Yearly trends.

Regional Insights → Sales performance by region/state.

Product Analysis → Top & Bottom performing vehicle categories.

Customer Insights → Segment contribution (Retail, Corporate, Fleet).

Dealer Performance → Revenue and sales distribution by dealer.

**4. Visualizations**

KPI Cards → Revenue, Units Sold, Avg Selling Price.

Line Chart → Sales/Revenue trends over time.

Bar/Column Charts → Sales by Category, Model, and Dealer.

Map Visualization → Regional sales contribution.

Donut Charts → Customer segmentation and contribution.

**Business Value**

This dashboard enables the automobile company to:

Monitor revenue growth across different time periods.

Identify high-performing vs underperforming products and regions.

Track dealer contributions to overall sales.

Understand customer segments driving the most revenue.

Support strategic decisions in pricing, product focus, and market expansion
